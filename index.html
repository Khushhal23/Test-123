Roadmap

GROWING SHROOMS

Our mission is to build a fun community of like minded individuals who will motivate you to become who you are meant to be in the metaverse. This project is designed to bring fun and excitement to the Solana ecosystem.

LAUNCH & SECONDARY MARKET LISTING

Once all 600 Shrooms have found a new home inside your wallets, they will be listed on all popular secondary marketplaces.

UTILITY TOKEN LAUNCH & STAKING

Shroom holders will have the option to receive daily staking rewards of our utility token "MUSH". There are three species of Shroom. Acid Shrooms, Stoner Shrooms and DMT Shrooms. Acid Shrooms get 10 daily MUSH, Stoner Shrooms gets 15 daily MUSH, and DMT Shrooms get 20 daily MUSH. Shroom holders need to acumulate 300 MUSH in order to initiate the Shroom Mutation.

MUTATION

Earn a total of 300 MUSH in order to send your Shroom away for a Mutation retreat. Your original Shroom will be burned and you will receive a Mutant Shroom in return.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Mushroom Garden Clubhouse?

Mushroom Garden Clubhouse is a collection of 600 Shrooms traveling through dimentions on the Solana blockchain. Stake your Shroom to earn daily rewards of MUSH Token. Earn enough MUSH token to transform your mushroom into an extremly disturbing Mutant Shroom!

Will there be whitelist spots available?

Yes, there are 100 WL spots available. Join our Discord server more details on how to get your WL spot.

When will I be able to mint a Shroom?

Mushroom Garden Clubhouse will launch on the 10th March 20:00 UTC.

How much will it cost to mint a Shroom?

It will cost 0.4 Solana to mint a Shroom. We reccomend to have at least 0.5 Solana in your wallet to cover gass fees.

Is this project on the Solana blockchain?

Yes we are proud to launch this project on the Solana blockchain.

Will you be listing on a secondary market place?

The Shrooms will be listed on all major secondary marketplaces.

Roadmap

GROWING SHROOMS

Our mission is to build a fun community of like minded individuals who will motivate you to become who you are meant to be in the metaverse. This project is designed to bring fun and excitement to the Solana ecosystem.

LAUNCH & SECONDARY MARKET LISTING

Once all 600 Shrooms have found a new home inside your wallets, they will be listed on all popular secondary marketplaces.

UTILITY TOKEN LAUNCH & STAKING

Shroom holders will have the option to receive daily staking rewards of our utility token "MUSH". There are three species of Shroom. Acid Shrroms, Stoner Shrooms and DMT Shrooms. Acid Shrooms get 10 daily MUSH, Stoner Shrooms gets 15 daily MUSH, and DMT Shrooms get 20 daily MUSH. Shroom holders need to acumulate 300 MUSH in order to initiate the Shroom Mutation.

MUTATION

Earn a total of 300 MUSH in order to send your Shroom away for a Mutation retreat. Your original Shroom will be burned and you will receive a Mutant Shroom in return.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are Bored Llamas?

Bored Llamas are a collection of 500 NFTs wasting time on the Solana blockchain. These Llamas are a little different though.

When will I be able to mint a Bored Llama?

The official launch date is on the 27th January 2022 at 20:00 UTC. The Whitelist pre-sale will go live at 19:00 UTC.

How much will it cost to mint a Bored Llama?

The mint price is 0.4 Solana.

Is this project on the Solana blockchain?

Yes we are proud to launch this project on the Solana blockchain.

Will you be listing on a secondary market place?

Bored Llamas will be listed on Solanart once the collection sells out. We have also had preliminary approval from Magic Eden.

Will there be Whitelist spots available?

There are 100 WL spots available. Check the pinned message in the announcements channel in our Discord server for more details.
